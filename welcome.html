What's WordUp Extension

As you browse the web, you can complete your WordUp knowledge map. The page you're viewing will be scanned by WordUp, and every word will be checked against your knowledge map.

-Blue underline means you should decide if you know this word.

Move your mouse over the blue line to see this icon.

How it works

WordUp extension helps you in two ways.

Firstly, it will help you to complete your WordUp knowledge map. Additionally, once your knowledge map is complete, it will cleverly highlight any word you do not know on any webpage, and help you look it up!

-Red underline means you should learn this word.

Move your mouse over the red line to see this icon.
